Burger

What is a Burger?

A burger is a dish consisting of ground beef, cooked in a patty, and typically served on a bun. Burgers are often garnished with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments such as ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.

Ingredients

FOR THE BURGERS

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

FOR THE ASSEMBLY

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 tomato slices
  • 4 pickle slices
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise, to taste

PREPARATION STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, milk, egg, onion, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Form the beef mixture into four patties.
  4. Place the patties on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through.
  5. While the burgers are baking, warm the hamburger buns.
  6. To assemble the burgers, place each patty on a bun. Top with cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickle, and condiments of your choice.
  7. Serve immediately.
